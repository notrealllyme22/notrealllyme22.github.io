About Us

Established in 1995, ANB has been a trusted name in the import and export of used trucks, spare parts, and heavy equipment. With over 25 years of experience, we have developed a reputation for delivering reliable, high-quality vehicles and parts sourced from Japan to countries across Africa, Yemen, Iraq, Pakistan, and Afghanistan.

  • Expertise: With over two decades of experience in importing and exporting trucks and parts, we have built a network of trusted suppliers and customers.

  • Quality Assurance: We take pride in the quality of the products we offer. Our trucks and equipment are sourced directly from reputable Japanese manufacturers.

  • Customer Service: Our dedicated team of 9 employees is always available to assist with your inquiries, provide advice, and ensure that your orders are delivered on time.

  • Global Reach: We have successfully exported to a wide range of countries, ensuring that our products are accessible across multiple regions.
